# Landingpage Conversion Review

When invoked:
1. Evaluate hero section clarity:
   - Is the problem clear?
   - Is the solution clear?
   - Is the CTA obvious?
2. Check CTA flow across the page.
3. Identify friction in user journey.
4. Improve trust signals and credibility.
5. Ensure content supports conversion, not just information.
6. Suggest structural improvements where needed.
